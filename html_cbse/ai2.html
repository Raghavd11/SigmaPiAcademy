Artificial Intelligence – CLASS XI
Unit Topics
Part A: Employability Skills
• Unit 1: Communication Skills-I (10 hours)
• Unit 2: Self-management Skills-I (10 hours)
• Unit 3: Information and Communication Technology Skills-I (10 hours)
• Unit 4: Entrepreneurial Skills-I (15 hours)
• Unit 5: Green Skills-I (5 hours)
Total Duration: 50 hours
Note: Detailed topics can be downloaded from the CBSE website.
Unit 1: Artificial Intelligence for Everyone
• Definition and history of Artificial Intelligence.
• Types and domains of AI.
• Key terminologies: machine learning, deep learning.
• Benefits and limitations of AI in various contexts.
Unit 2: Unlocking Your Future in AI
• Global demand for AI professionals.
• Career opportunities in AI across industries.
• Required skills and tools for AI careers.
• Personal evaluation of interests and career pathways in AI.
Unit 3: Python Programming
• Basics of Python: character sets, tokens, modes, operators, and data types.
• Control statements: conditional and iterative.
• Python libraries: NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn.
• Working with CSV files.
Unit 4: Introduction to Capstone Project
• Problem decomposition using 5W1H.
• Introduction to design thinking and empathy mapping.
• Alignment with Sustainable Development Goals.
• Non-technical communication of AI project solutions.
Unit 5: Data Literacy – Data Collection to Data Analysis
• Introduction to data literacy and data collection methods.
• Exploring and analyzing data.
• Statistical analysis: mean, median, mode, standard deviation.
• Data visualization techniques.
• Introduction to matrices and data preprocessing.
Unit 6: Machine Learning Algorithms
• Overview of machine learning and its types.
• Supervised learning: correlation, regression, linear regression, classification (k-NN).
• Unsupervised learning: clustering and k-means algorithm.
Unit 7: Leveraging Linguistics and Computer Science
• Human language complexity and its challenges for NLP.
• Introduction to NLP: emotion detection, sentiment analysis, classification, chatbots.
• Phases and applications of NLP.
Unit 8: AI Ethics and Values
• Principles of ethics in AI.
• Understanding bias in AI and its implications.
• Strategies to mitigate bias and promote fairness.
• Importance of AI policies and regulatory frameworks.
Artificial Intelligence – CLASS XII
Unit Topics
Unit 1: Capstone Project
• Understanding the problem and decomposing it using the Design Thinking framework.
• Formulating an analytical approach and identifying data requirements.
• Introduction to modelling approaches.
• Validating model quality using test-train split and cross-validation.
• Evaluation metrics: RMSE, MSE, MAPE with examples.
• Overview of commonly used algorithms and the science behind them.
• Presenting findings through a compelling narrative.
Unit 2: Model Lifecycle
• Understanding different aspects of a model: training, testing, and validation.
• Introduction to hyperparameters.
• Overview of platforms and tools for building and running models.
• Lifecycle stages of an AI model: Build, Deploy, Retrain.
Unit 3: Storytelling Through Data
• Importance of storytelling in data communication and decision-making.
• Structuring a story: beginning with purpose, roadmap, and conclusion.
• Ethical considerations in storytelling.
• Choosing appropriate charts for data types:
– Wordclouds for text
– Facet grids for mixed data
– Line/bar charts for numeric data
– Candlestick charts for stock data
– Maps for geographic data
• Role of storytelling at different stages of predictive modeling:
– Data exploration
– Feature visualization
– Model creation
– Model comparison
